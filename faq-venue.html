Frequently Asked Questions - GATEWAYS 2026

Department of Computer Science, Christ (Deemed to be University), Bangalore

What are the registration fees for the events?

Registration fees vary by event:

  • Technical Track Pass: ₹200
  • Gaming Track Pass: ₹150
  • Cultural Track Pass: ₹100
  • All-access Pass: ₹400

All payments are to be made at the Department Office during working hours.

How do I register for multiple events?

You can register for multiple events during the registration process. Select your preferred events on the registration form. Each track pass allows entry to all events within that track.

Is accommodation provided for outstation participants?

Yes, accommodation is available for outstation participants at nominal rates. The university guest house facilities can be availed by sending a request at least one week in advance.

What is the last date for registration?

The last date for registration is April 10, 2026. Late registrations will be subject to availability. Register early to secure your spot!

Are there prizes for winners?

Yes! Prizes include cash awards, certificates, and internship opportunities with our industry partners including CISCO, REDHAT, ORACLE Academy, and TCS.

Can I volunteer at the event?

We welcome volunteers from the Department of Computer Science! Contact gateways@christuniversity.in or call 080-40129100 for volunteer opportunities.

What facilities are available at the venue?

The Computer Science Building has state-of-the-art laboratories, high-speed internet, air-conditioned halls, and dedicated networking labs for technical events.

Who can participate in the events?

All Christ University students from undergraduate and postgraduate programs are eligible. Students from other universities can participate with prior approval from the HOD.
